About the Author

James Tolliver writes novels about the weight men carry—the responsibilities they accept, the silences they inherit, and the moments that demand honesty.

His stories explore fatherhood, service, duty, and the quiet search for connection, loyalty, and meaning in a fast-moving world.

He lives and writes in Northern Alabama.

About the Novel

By Patrons

By Patrons is a novel about fathers and sons, inherited ambition, and the quiet damage that can pass from one generation of men to the next without ever being named. Set against a cross-country drive to a son’s graduation, it explores family, service, duty, and what it costs to spend a life chasing approval in the language your father taught you to understand.

At its heart, By Patrons is about whether a man can break a pattern that shaped the men before him, and whether love can still be spoken plainly before it is too late.
